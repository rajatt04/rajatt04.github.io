The Overview

A comprehensive digital solution designed to digitize classroom communications. StudyMate bridges the gap between faculty and students, centralizing records, seamless assignment delivery, and instant critical notice broadcasting.


account_tree Scalable Cloud Architecture

Real-time Synchronization

Implemented Firebase Realtime DB listeners to ensure notices arrive to students instantly without refreshing.

Role-based Security

Leveraged Firebase Custom Claims to securely route users to either the Faculty Dashboard or Student Hub upon login.
